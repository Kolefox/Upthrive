About Upthrive

I built Upthrive because I kept seeing the same problem: businesses investing in marketing but not in systems. They'd run ads without a follow-up process. They'd launch a website without a conversion path. They'd generate leads that disappeared into a spreadsheet no one managed. The inputs were there. The infrastructure wasn't.

My background is in Finance and Computer Science — I graduated from Florida Atlantic University with a Bachelor's in Finance and a minor in CS. Early in my career, I worked at an investment bank where I led the company's automation initiatives. We built systems that eliminated manual workflows, reduced errors, and freed the team to focus on higher-value work. The efficiency gains were significant, and the lesson was clear: the right system, built right, changes the output.

That experience is the foundation of Upthrive. I brought the operator mindset from finance — where every dollar is accounted for, every process has a purpose — and combined it with the technical ability to actually build the infrastructure. The result is an agency that doesn't just run campaigns or design sites in isolation. We build connected growth systems where traffic feeds conversion, conversion feeds a CRM, and the CRM feeds automation that keeps working after hours.

Today, I work with business owners across South Florida to audit their current setup, identify where the highest-leverage improvements are, and build the systems that turn marketing spend into compounding revenue. The work is methodical, data-driven, and built to last. That's what Upthrive was built to deliver.

Upthrive was built to help businesses scale smarter.

Kole Fox Founder, Upthrive
The Thesis

Why Upthrive exists.

The standard agency model sells you a deliverable — a website, a set of ads, an SEO retainer — and hands it off. You manage the pieces. You figure out how they connect. You handle what falls through the cracks.

Upthrive was built to reject that model. Every service we offer is designed to plug into a connected system: traffic flows into landing pages built to convert, leads flow into a CRM that organizes and follows up automatically, and performance data flows back into every decision we make next. Nothing is isolated. Every layer compounds the one before it.

The businesses that win consistently aren't the ones with the biggest budgets — they're the ones with the best systems. That's what we build.

How We Operate

Four things we don't compromise on.

These aren't slogans. They're the operating principles behind every client engagement, every campaign, every system we build.

01

Systems over tactics.

Tactics produce results once. Systems produce results repeatedly. We build infrastructure that works when you're not looking — not one-off fixes that need constant attention to maintain.

02

Data over opinion.

Every recommendation we make is grounded in what the numbers say. We don't guess what creative will perform or which audience will convert. We test, measure, and follow the data — even when it surprises us.

03

Compounding over flash.

We optimize for long-term compounding returns, not short-term wins that look good in a screenshot. The work we do in month one should still be paying dividends in month twelve.

04

Operator mindset.

We think like business owners, not marketers. Every decision we make is weighed against the actual cost and the actual return — because the goal isn't to look busy, it's to move the number that matters.

Where we work.

Upthrive is based in South Florida and works with businesses throughout the region — from Miami to Palm Beach and across the I-95 corridor. We're also fully remote-capable and work with clients outside South Florida who want a systems-driven approach to growth.

If your market is local, we know the landscape. If it's national, we build for scale.

What working with us looks like.

  • A full audit of your current setup before any work begins — no assumptions
  • A clear strategy document with priorities ranked by impact
  • Weekly reporting with plain-language interpretation, not just numbers
  • Direct access — no account managers passing messages back and forth
  • Honest timelines and honest expectations about what each channel can do
