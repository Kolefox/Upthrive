Meet the Founder

About Upthrive

I built Upthrive to help businesses grow through a proprietary system powered by custom tools, software, and automation developed to solve real operational problems.

I graduated from Florida Atlantic University with a Bachelor's in Finance and a minor in Computer Science. Early in my career, I worked at an investment bank where I led much of the company's automation work, creating systems that reduced manual tasks, improved efficiency, and removed a large amount of repetitive work from the team.

That experience shaped the foundation of Upthrive. I saw firsthand how the right systems can transform a business — not just by saving time, but by creating a stronger, more scalable operation.

Today, I work with business owners to audit their company, identify weak points, implement better systems, and use modern automation and AI to reduce costs, increase efficiency, and drive more revenue.

Upthrive was built to help businesses scale smarter.

Kole Fox Founder, Upthrive
